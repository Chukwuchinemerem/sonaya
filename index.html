Meet The Founder

Ozoemena Maryann Mmesoma

Maryann is the founder and lead lash & brow artist behind Sonaya Beauty Studio, with two years of dedicated, hands-on experience across the full range of lash extension systems and semi-permanent brow techniques. Her approach is deliberate rather than trend-chasing: every set and every brow shape is mapped to the client's individual eye shape, bone structure and natural growth pattern before any product is applied.

Based in Enugu State, Maryann also travels for private home-service appointments across Abuja, Delta, Lagos and Anambra states — bringing the full studio experience directly to clients who prefer to be styled in the comfort of their own space.

  • Trained across classic, hybrid, volume and mega volume lash systems
  • Specialises in ombre, combo, microshading, microblading & nano brows
  • 150+ clients served across five Nigerian states
